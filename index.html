PLASTIC POLLUTION

Plastic is a synthetic product made of long chains of atoms of carbon (polymers). Its strength and versatility made it an essential material in our life.
However, if you disperse plastic in the environment it can be a source of pollution, in particular for oceans (about 80%). Every year, we can find from about 4 to 12 tons of plastic in oceans like bottles, bags, packaging. These materials are eaten by marine animals since they think plastic is a prey so they die from suffocation. This problem has a solution: recycling. After you use plastic, in fact, you can melt it and create new objects.
